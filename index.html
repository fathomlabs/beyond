Maasai Mara Safari

Travel into the rift valley and experience the magical Maasai Mara Reserve first hand with a local Maasai guide. Custom build a safari to match your interests: follow the big cats as they hunt and eat; have a picnic breakfast next to the Mara rive watching hippos and crocodiles; head out to the hills to see rhino and check off the big five; get up close and personal with the friendly cheetah; and enjoy the huge diversity of plant, birds, reptiles, frogs, and insects. What ever you enjoy, plan an experience with us that will stay with you forever.
